● About MSFDA

A Unique Initiative for Quality Higher Education

A unique initiative of the Government of Maharashtra towards ensuring quality higher education to develop good, thoughtful, well-rounded, creative individuals and nurturing colleges as safe and vibrant spaces through capacity building programs for teachers and other stakeholders from universities and colleges.

● Vision & Values

Vision & Values

🎯

AIM

"Quality higher education to develop good thoughtful, well-rounded and creative individuals"

🔭

VISION

"Transforming higher educational landscape, through continuous professional development of teachers in HEIs of Maharashtra"

🧠

Learning Beyond Knowledge

Promote critical thinking, co-learning, congenial learning environment, free from fear and competition.

🔬

Scientific & Rational Thinking

Develop scientific temper, humanism, spirit of enquiry and reform.

🌍

Diversity & Inclusion

Promote diversity and inclusion as a non-negotiable fundamental principle in all aspects of higher education.

🌱

Experiential Learning & Innovation

Adopt pedagogies that promote discovery, questioning and experiential learning by students.
